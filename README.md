# KE Designs Website

A modern, responsive website for KE Designs - offering Photography, IoT Product Consulting, and Business Process Consulting services.

## Business Services

KE Designs provides three specialized services:

1. **Photography**: Professional photography services specializing in sports photography, wedding photography, and portrait sessions
2. **IoT Product Consulting**: Expert consulting to help create innovative products with IoT capabilities using Particle microcontrollers
3. **Business Process Consulting**: Google Workspace migration, task management implementation, time tracking solutions, and comprehensive training

## Features

- **Modern Design**: Clean, professional design with smooth animations
- **Fully Responsive**: Works beautifully on all devices (desktop, tablet, mobile)
- **Smooth Navigation**: Fixed navbar with smooth scrolling
- **Interactive Elements**: Hover effects, animations, and transitions
- **Contact Form**: Ready-to-use contact form (backend integration needed)
- **Portfolio Section**: Showcase your work with an elegant grid layout organized by service category
- **Service Cards**: Three main service cards with detailed feature lists

## Sections

1. **Hero Section**: Eye-catching landing area highlighting the three service areas
2. **About**: Information about your business and the three service offerings
3. **Services**: Three main service cards (Photography, IoT Consulting, Business Consulting) with feature lists
4. **Portfolio**: Gallery organized by service type (Photography, IoT, Business Consulting)
5. **Contact**: Contact form and business information
6. **Footer**: Links and social media

## Customization

### Colors
Edit the CSS variables in `styles.css` to change the color scheme:
```css
:root {
    --primary-color: #6366f1;
    --primary-dark: #4f46e5;
    --secondary-color: #8b5cf6;
    /* ... */
}
```

### Content
- Update text content in `index.html`
- Replace portfolio placeholders with your actual project images:
  - Sports, wedding, and portrait photography samples
  - IoT product development projects
  - Business process consulting case studies
- Update contact information (email, phone, location)
- Update statistics in the About section to reflect your actual numbers

### Images
Replace portfolio placeholders with your actual project images. The portfolio section is organized into three categories:
- **Photography**: Sports, weddings, portraits
- **IoT Consulting**: Product development projects
- **Business Consulting**: Workspace migrations and process improvements

Update the HTML structure in the portfolio section to use `<img>` tags instead of placeholders.
